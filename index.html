What does Grey Shore Systems actually do?+
We design and build backend automation systems for service businesses. Things like follow-up sequences, client onboarding, review generation, CRM integrations, and any repetitive process your team does manually today.
Do I need to learn new software?+
No. We plug into the tools you already use. CRM, calendar, email, billing. Your workflow stays the same. It just runs faster and without the manual work.
How long does setup take?+
Most systems are live within 2-3 weeks. We handle the entire build. You don't need technical knowledge.
Do you work with my specific tools?+
We work with most CRMs and business tools. GoHighLevel, PushPress, HubSpot, Google Workspace, Shopify, and many more. During our discovery call, we'll map out exactly how the systems integrate with what you're already using.
How much does it cost?+
Pricing is based on the complexity of the build and how many systems are involved. Book a call and we'll give you a straight answer in 15 minutes.
Will this replace my team?+
No. Automation handles the repetitive, time-consuming tasks your team dreads. That frees them up for the work that actually moves the needle: relationships, complex decisions, and revenue.
What kind of results can I expect?+
Most clients see 10-30 hours saved per week and recover thousands in revenue from missed leads and forgotten follow-ups. You'll see the impact within the first month.
